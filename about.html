Bio

My journey didn’t begin in a lab — it began with a phone and curiosity. At 16, I took my first freelance project and failed. That failure became my teacher. I learned marketing, then code, then product. I shipped small, learned fast, and kept going.

In college, I chose AI & Data Science to build the future hands‑on. I co‑founded Z1Renders, earned clients, and built our first PC with revenue. Later I launched Z2Learn to teach the way I learn — by building. Today, with Z3Connect, I’m focused on affordable, reliable SaaS that helps MSMEs scale.

"Make it simple. Make it reliable. Make it accessible."
