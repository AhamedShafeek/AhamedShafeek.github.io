My Journey

From Freelancer to Founder

A timeline of failures, pivots, and small wins that led to building two startups in India's SaaS and EdTech space.

2021 • Age 16

The Spark

First freelance project: video editing with just a mobile phone

Failed fast, discovered my love for building. The best education came from early failures.

2021—2022

Digital Marketing Deep Dive

Mastered affiliate marketing and growth systems

Studied consumer behavior and funnels while balancing school — foundation for product growth later.

2022 • Age 17

AI & Data Science

Chose AI as ChatGPT era began

Wanted to be where the future was built — not where the past was taught.

2023

First Startup — Z1Renders

3D rendering and animation studio

Earned first capital, learned Unity, and the realities of operations. Pivoted after validating market fit.

2023

Z2Learn Launch

Project‑based EdTech

Taught 600+ students through 30+ workshops. Built hands‑on AI and dev courses for schools and colleges.

2024

Z3Connect Born

AI‑powered SaaS for MSMEs

India‑first approach: Z CRM, Z Assist, Z Booking, Z ERP, and more — affordable tools that work together.

2024—2025

Product Marathon

22+ tools built in stealth

Designed an integrated ecosystem with shared auth, data, and UI patterns for seamless adoption.

2025 — Present

Pre‑Launch Readiness

50+ businesses onboarded

Hardening infra, polishing UX, and preparing for a 2026 ecosystem release.

2026 • Vision

The Big Launch

Full ecosystem release + ZUniversity

Where students graduate with portfolios and MSMEs scale with AI — accessible innovation for everyone.

Key Learnings

Fail Fast, Learn Faster

Every failure taught me more than any success. The video editing disaster at 16 showed me I had grit. Z1Renders taught me about market fit. Each setback was a setup for the next breakthrough.

India First, Global Later

Everyone wants to go global immediately. I chose to master the Indian market first. 63 million MSMEs need affordable solutions. Win here, then expand.

Build in Stealth

3 years of quiet building. No hype, no announcements. Just focused execution. When we launch, we'll be ready to scale—not scrambling to build.

Pivot is Growth

From video editing to marketing to 3D to education to SaaS. Each pivot wasn't failure—it was evolution. Stay flexible, stay learning.
