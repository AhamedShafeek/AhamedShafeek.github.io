🎯 My Story

From a School Kid with a Phone to Leading 3 Startups

Started my entrepreneurial journey at 15 with just a smartphone and burning passion. Founded Z1Renders (3D rendering & animation), learned Unity, business fundamentals, and built the capital to fuel my bigger dreams.

Today, Z3Connect powers businesses across India with AI-driven SaaS tools like Z CRM, Z Assist, Z Booking, Z ERP, and Z Content Creator. Meanwhile, Z2Learn has taught 600+ students through hands-on, project-based courses in AI, coding, no-code, and game development.

Tech Stack: React.js, Node.js, Flutter, Python, MongoDB, Firebase, Express.js, Flask, and more.
